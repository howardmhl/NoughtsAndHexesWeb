Noughts & Hexes
Quick guide to casting curses in Noughts & Hexes.

How to Play

Noughts & Hexes is a magical twist on noughts and crosses. Place tokens, and use skills to bend the board in your favour.

  • Goal: make a line of five of your pieces.
  • Your pieces fade away after 14 turns, so plan carefully.
  • Each witch has a special skill that charges over time.
  • Use skills to disrupt, defend, or force tricky moves.
  • Tap a tile to place a piece.
  • Tap your skill button to activate a spell.

Witches & Skills

Each colour represents a witch with her own signature spell:

Pink Witch
Convert one enemy piece.
Blue Witch
Protect a piece with a bubble shield.
Yellow Witch
Move any piece to an empty tile.
Purple Witch
Curse a piece so it jumps around.
Red Witch
Remove any piece completely.
Orange Witch
Force your opponent to play in a tile you choose.

Hidden Magic

Some witches only appear to those who know the old spell.
